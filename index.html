Project Overview

Project RACCOON is a cutting-edge, multi-function environmental PocketQube satellite designed for the Qube2Space 2024 competition. Our mission is to push the boundaries of space-based environmental monitoring and data collection using advanced sensors and AI-driven analysis. This website provides a detailed overview of our mission objectives, system architecture, and power consumption.
