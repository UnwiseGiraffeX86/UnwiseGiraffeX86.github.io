Technical Specifications

The Project RACCOON satellite is equipped with an array of advanced sensors and technologies, designed to perform precise environmental monitoring and data collection. These cutting-edge components allow the satellite to capture critical atmospheric data, high-resolution imagery, and thermal information, while also employing AI-driven analysis to detect anomalies in real-time.

Microcontroller

Powered by the ESP32-S3, enabling efficient data processing and real-time anomaly detection through machine learning algorithms.

Atmospheric Monitoring

  • BME680 Sensor: Measures atmospheric temperature, barometric pressure, humidity, AQI, and VOC.
  • ICM-20600 IMU: Captures acceleration data on three axes for precise movement and orientation tracking.
  • SGP41-D-R4 Sensor: Monitors NOx and CO levels.
  • GUVA S12SD Sensor: Provides accurate UV light intensity measurements.

Imaging Technology

  • OV2640 Camera: Captures high-resolution visual imagery for detailed Earth observation.
  • AMG8833 Thermal Camera: Adds thermal imaging capabilities, allowing for temperature-based environmental insights.

Communication & Navigation

  • GY NEO6MV2 GPS: Ensures accurate satellite positioning and navigation data.
  • RFM96 LoRa Module: Ensures long-range data transmission between the satellite and ground stations.
